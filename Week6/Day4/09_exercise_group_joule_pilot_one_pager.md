# Group exercise: Joule pilot “one-pager”

**2 groups**, **2–3 members** each. Roles: **Facilitator**, **Recorder**, **Presenter**.

No SAP access—produce a **one-page brief** (bullets are fine) you could give to a project sponsor.

---

## Shared customer sketch

**Northwind Retail** has:

- 2,000 store employees (high turnover, many casual users)
- A centralized HR policy library (often outdated PDFs)
- A finance team that fears incorrect postings during month-end

Leadership says: “Install AI everywhere by Friday.”

---

## Deliverable (both groups)

Create a **one-pager** with these sections:

1. **Goal** (one sentence)
2. **Pilot scope** (what is in / out)
3. **Top 3 use cases** (each with primary **interaction pattern**)
4. **Risks + mitigations** (at least 4 bullets total)
5. **Success metrics** (at least 2 measurable)
6. **Next step in week 1** (one concrete workshop or decision)

---

## Group differentiation (to avoid duplicate outputs)

- **Group 1 — Employee experience first**
  Prioritize **informational** + **navigational** patterns for store users. You must include **document grounding** assumptions (what documents must be cleaned up first).

- **Group 2 — Controlled automation first**
  Prioritize **transactional** patterns only where oversight is explicit. You must include a **month-end “red line”** list: what Joule must **not** do during close.

---

## Present back (3 minutes each)

Answer:

- What did you **not** include in the pilot—and why?
- What is the **single biggest** governance decision the sponsor must make?
