# Day 5: Your consultant toolkit (what you can apply next)
## A practical checklist for new SAP projects

This learning file turns the program into an **action toolkit** you can use on a new customer project.

---

## Learning objectives

By the end, you will be able to:
- Create a simple “consultant toolkit” across discovery, design, implementation, and AI adoption
- Identify the evidence you should produce at each stage
- Decide what to ask for when information is missing
