# Group exercise: 6-week learning map
## 2 groups, 2–3 members each (paper workshop)

No SAP access required. This is a consolidation activity.

---

## Roles

- **Facilitator** – keeps the group moving
- **Recorder** – captures the answers
- **Presenter** – shares back

---

## Group assignments

- **Group 1 — Weeks 1–3 (foundations + design thinking)**
- **Group 2 — Weeks 4–6 (technical design + implementation + Joule + wrap-up)**

---
