## Part 3: Functional Knowledge â€“ What Does It Mean?

In SAP, **functional knowledge** means understanding **business processes and how SAP supports them**, not only technical installation or programming.

### Three Main Areas

**1. ERP Systems**
- **ERP** = Enterprise Resource Planning: software that helps companies manage core business areas (finance, HR, sales, procurement, etc.) in one system.
- **Functional knowledge** here = knowing what finance, HR, or sales *do* in the company and how SAP supports those processes.

**2. Cross-Functional Integration**
- Different SAP modules (Finance, Sales, HR, etc.) work together.
- **Functional knowledge** = understanding how data and processes flow *between* these areas (e.g. a sale in Sales creates an invoice in Finance).

**3. Industry Solutions**
- SAP offers **industry-specific** solutions (e.g. for automotive, retail, healthcare).
- **Functional knowledge** = knowing the typical processes and needs of an industry and how SAP addresses them.
