# Day 3: Group Exercise – Standard vs Extend vs Custom
## Two Groups, Two Decision Sets

Work in **2 groups**. Each group evaluates scenarios and recommends whether to use **standard**, **extend**, or **custom**.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – keeps time and guides the discussion
  - **Recorder** – captures notes
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~5 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

---

## 🎯 Task Overview

Each group gets a different decision set:

- **Group 1:** “Keep standard” bias scenarios (optimize for maintainability)  
- **Group 2:** “Extend or custom” scenarios (optimize for business value)  

For each scenario:
1. Decide **S / E / C**:
   - **S** = Standard (configure)
   - **E** = Extend standard (keep core clean)
   - **C** = Custom build
2. Give **2 pros** and **2 cons**
3. Mention one **upgrade / Simplification List** concern

Use:
- `02_learning_fit_to_standard_vs_custom.md`
- `03_exercise_fiori_and_standard.md`
