# Day 1: Group Exercise – Design Documents and Stakeholders
## Two Groups, Two Document Sets

Work in **2 groups**. Each group looks at a different **set of design documents** and discusses who uses them and why they matter.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – guides the discussion and keeps time
  - **Recorder** – captures notes
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~5 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

---

## 🎯 Task Overview

Each group focuses on one side of the design documentation:

- **Group 1:** **Process and functional documents**  
- **Group 2:** **Technical and integration documents**  

For your document set:
1. List **key document types** (3–5 items)
2. Describe briefly **what each document is for**
3. Note **who uses it** (roles, not names)

Use ideas from:
- `02_learning_technical_design_documentation.md`
- Week 3 design and blueprint sessions

## 📢 Presenting to the Class

Each **Presenter** should share in about **5 minutes**:
1. Your **document set** (process/functional or technical/integration)
2. The **key documents** you identified
3. One or two **roles** that depend heavily on those documents

Together, the two groups should give a complete picture of who uses which documents and why they are important in an SAP project.
