# Day 2: Daily Stand-up & Communication Focus
## Stand-up, Sprint Mindset, and Today’s Focus

Start Day 2 with a short **stand‑up** and a reminder that clear communication is as important as good design.

---

## Stand-up Questions (Same Structure)

Answer the three standard stand‑up questions:

1. **What did I accomplish since the last stand‑up?**  
2. **What am I planning to do next (today)?**  
3. **Is anything blocking my progress?**  

You can do this alone (self‑check) or in a small group (2–3 minutes each).
