# Day 2: Group Exercise – Stakeholder Pitch Scenarios
## Two Groups, Two Stakeholder Angles

Work in **2 groups**. Each group prepares a short **design pitch plan** for different stakeholder perspectives.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – guides the discussion and keeps time
  - **Recorder** – captures key points
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~5 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

---

## 🎯 Task Overview

You are preparing to present an SAP solution design.

- **Group 1:** Focus on **Executive board + Business users**  
- **Group 2:** Focus on **IT / Security / Architecture**  

For your stakeholder set:
1. Decide **what to emphasize** in your design pitch
2. Plan **how to run the review** (structure and flow)
3. Prepare a short **pitch outline** to present to the class

Use ideas from:
- `01_learning_communicating_pitching_designs.md`
- `03_learning_design_review.md`

## 📢 Presenting to the Class

Each **Presenter** should share in about **5 minutes**:
1. Your **stakeholder set**
2. The **main points** you would emphasize in your pitch
3. Your **brief pitch outline**

Try to keep your explanation clear enough that someone who hasn’t seen the design before can follow the story.
