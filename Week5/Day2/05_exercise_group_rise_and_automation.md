# Group Exercise: RISE + Testing Strategy Recommendations
## 2 Groups, 2–3 Members Each

Form **2 groups** (2–3 members each). Assign roles:
- **Facilitator** – keeps the group focused and on time  
- **Recorder** – writes the deliverable clearly  
- **Presenter** – presents back to the class  

Timebox:
- **15 minutes** prepare
- **3–5 minutes** per group presentation

---

## Customer Prompt (Same for Both Groups)

A mid-size company is moving to the cloud with **RISE with SAP**. They have:
- A tight timeline
- Some integrations (CRM + HR)
- Worry about stability and adoption after go‑live

Your job: give the customer **3 clear recommendations** plus a simple “why.”

---

## Group 1 – RISE + Cloud Operations View

Deliverable: a short “RISE success checklist” with **3 recommendations**.

Include recommendations about:
- How to keep the project **fit‑to‑standard**
- Where **BTP / extensions** might be needed (and how to avoid over-customizing)
- What must be clarified early for **operational readiness** (support model, roles, hypercare expectations)

## Group 2 – Testing, Monitoring, and Automation View

Deliverable: a short “quality and stability plan” with **3 recommendations**.

Include recommendations about:
- A practical **testing strategy** (what types of testing matter first)
- A practical **monitoring focus** for go‑live and hypercare
- Where **environment automation** helps reduce delay and risk (e.g., refreshes, repeatable deployments)

## Present Back (All Groups)

Your presenter should answer:
- What are your **3 recommendations**?
- What is the **main risk** you are reducing?
- What would you ask the customer next to confirm your plan?
