# Group Exercise: Lifecycle Phases for Different Stakeholders
## 2 Groups, 2–3 Members Each

Work in **2 groups** (2–3 members per group). Assign roles:
- **Facilitator** – keeps the group on time and on topic  
- **Recorder** – writes the group’s answers  
- **Presenter** – shares back in the class  

You have **15–20 minutes** to prepare, then **3–5 minutes** per group to present.

---

## Group Assignments

- **Group 1 – Project preparation + Blueprinting/Design**  
- **Group 2 – Realization + Final preparation + Go‑live and support**

Each group should think about:
- Key **activities**
- Key **deliverables**
- Which **stakeholders** are most involved
