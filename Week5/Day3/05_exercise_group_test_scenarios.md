# Group Exercise: Design Test Scenarios (Order-to-Cash vs Procure-to-Pay)
## 2 Groups, 2–3 Members Each

Form **2 groups** (2–3 members each). Assign roles:
- **Facilitator** – keeps the group on time and ensures coverage  
- **Recorder** – writes the scenarios and cases clearly  
- **Presenter** – shares back to the class  

Timebox:
- **20 minutes** prepare
- **3–5 minutes** per group presentation

---

## What You Must Produce (Deliverable)

For your process area, create:
- **3 test scenarios** (end-to-end)
- For each scenario: **3 test cases**
  - At least **1 positive** case
  - At least **1 negative** case
  - At least **1 edge** case

Keep it business-focused (what the user is trying to do) and add just enough technical detail (roles, data, integration points) to be testable.
