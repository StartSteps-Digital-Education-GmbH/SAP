## ğŸ’¡ Activity 2: Simple Robotics Explanation

### What is Robotics?

**Definition:** Robotics involves the design, construction, operation, and use of robots to perform tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously.

**What does this mean?**
- **Design and construction** = Making robots
- **Operation and use** = Using robots
- **Autonomously** = By themselves, without human help
- **Semi-autonomously** = Mostly by themselves, with some human help

**Simple Explanation:**
Robotics = Making and using robots that can do tasks by themselves!

**Examples:**
- Robot vacuum cleaner
- Factory assembly robots
- Medical surgery robots
- Delivery robots

**Can you think of another example?**
> self driving car

## ğŸ§© Activity 4: What is Automation?

### Simple Explanation

**Definition:** Automation involves using technology to perform tasks with minimal human intervention.

**What does this mean?**
- **Using technology** = Computers, software, robots
- **Perform tasks** = Do work
- **Minimal human intervention** = Little or no human help needed

**Simple Example:**
- **Manual:** You wake up, walk to coffee maker, press button, wait
- **Automated:** Coffee maker starts at 7 AM automatically

**Another Example:**
- **Manual:** You check email every hour
- **Automated:** Email app checks for you and notifies you

**Can you think of another example?**
> Lights that turn on when we enter a room

## ğŸ® Activity 6: Automation vs. Manual

### Compare the Two

**Task:** Paying a bill

**Manual Way:**
1. Receive the paper or email bill.
2. Go to the bank
3. Enter payment details
4. Save or print the receipt.

**Automated Way:**
1. Activate automatic payment (set up once).
2. Choose payment date
3. You receive a confirmation notification

**Which is easier? Why?**
> Automated- faster, saver and saves time
