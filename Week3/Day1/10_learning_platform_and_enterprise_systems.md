## Key Points: SAP Platform and Enterprise Systems

1. **Single environment for many processes**  
   The SAP platform (applications, HANA, Fiori, cloud, etc.) gives organizations one place to run finance, sales, HR, supply chain, and more – instead of many disconnected systems.

2. **Integration**  
   The platform is designed to connect SAP applications to each other and to non-SAP systems (APIs, integration tools, middleware), so data and processes flow across the enterprise.

3. **Scalability and performance**  
   With HANA and cloud offerings, the platform can scale to more users and more data while keeping performance and availability suitable for the business.

4. **User experience and adoption**  
   Fiori and modern UX make it easier for people to use SAP in their daily work, which supports adoption and efficiency across the enterprise.
