# Day 3: Group Exercise – Design Kickoff
## What Do You Already Know About Implementing SAP?

We start Day 3 with a quick **group warm‑up** about designing and implementing SAP solutions.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – keeps time and guides the discussion
  - **Recorder** – writes down answers
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~3 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

---

## 🎯 Task Overview

Each group answers **one key question** about designing and implementing an SAP solution.
You do **not** need technical details – focus on **ideas and common sense**.

Use your own experience and what you have seen so far in the program (Course 2, Course 3, Weeks 1–2).
