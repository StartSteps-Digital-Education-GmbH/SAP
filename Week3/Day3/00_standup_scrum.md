## 🚧 Blockers: What and How to Escalate

A **blocker** is anything that stops you from making progress (e.g. missing information, access issue, unclear requirement, waiting on someone).

**In a real stand-up:** If you have a blocker, you say it briefly. The Scrum Master or team then helps (e.g. “Let’s talk after the stand-up” or “I can unblock you by …”).

**Practice:** If you wrote a blocker above, add one sentence: **What would you do to escalate or resolve it?** (e.g. “Ask the trainer,” “Pair with a peer,” “Skip this task and do another first.”)
