# Day 3: Group Exercise – Discovery Workshop Roles
## Two Stakeholder Groups

Work in **2 groups**. Each group focuses on a **set of stakeholder roles** you might meet in discovery and design workshops.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – guides the discussion and keeps time
  - **Recorder** – captures notes
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~5 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

---

## 🎯 Task Overview

Each group takes a **stakeholder set**:

- **Group 1:** Executive / Senior Sponsor **and** Business Users / Process Owners  
- **Group 2:** IT / Security / Architecture  

For your stakeholder set:
1. Describe **what they care about** in an SAP design
2. Suggest **3–4 questions** you should ask them in a discovery workshop
3. Explain **what they need to see** in a design pitch or review

Use ideas from:
- `02_learning_designing_sap_solution.md`
- `04_learning_sap_activate.md`
- Your Coursera courses (especially Course 3)

## 📢 Presenting to the Class

Each **Presenter** should share in about **5 minutes**:
1. The **stakeholder set** your group focused on
2. The **top 2–3 things** they care about
3. A few of your **discovery questions**
