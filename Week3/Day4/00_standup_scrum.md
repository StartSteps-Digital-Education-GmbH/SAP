## 📋 Backlog and Definition of Done

In Scrum, the **backlog** is the list of work to do (prioritized). **Definition of Done (DoD)** is the checklist that defines when a task is “done” (e.g. “Reviewed,” “Tested,” “Documented”).

**Practice:** Look at today’s plan (learning + exercises). Pick **one item** from your “planning to do next” and write a **mini Definition of Done** for it (1–2 criteria). Example: “Done = I completed the exercise and wrote the reflection.”
