# Day 4: Group Exercise – Feedback to Re‑Design
## Two Groups, Two Feedback Scenarios

Work in **2 groups**. Each group receives **feedback** on a design and must propose changes.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – guides the discussion and keeps time
  - **Recorder** – captures notes
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~5 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

---

## 🎯 Task Overview

You are mid‑project and have just completed a design review.  
Each group gets **one type of feedback** about the proposed design.

- **Group 1:** “Too expensive / unclear value” and “Missing mobile”  
- **Group 2:** “Security concerns” and “Too complex for users”  

For your feedback set:
1. Summarize the **key concerns** in your own words
2. Propose **2–3 design changes** or clarifications
3. Plan how you would **communicate** those changes back to stakeholders

Use ideas from:
- `01_learning_communicating_pitching_designs.md`
- `03_learning_design_review.md`
- `05_learning_redesign_and_feedback.md`

## 📢 Presenting to the Class

Each **Presenter** should share in about **5 minutes**:
1. The **feedback set** your group worked on
2. Your **summary of concerns**
3. The **changes** you propose to the design
4. How you would **communicate** those changes back to stakeholders

Focus on showing how you use feedback to **improve** the design rather than defend the original idea.
