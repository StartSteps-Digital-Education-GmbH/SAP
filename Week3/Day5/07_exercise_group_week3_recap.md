# Day 5: Group Exercise – Week 3 Recap
## Two Groups, Two Recap Angles

Work in **2 groups** to recap what you learned in Week 3.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – guides the discussion and keeps time
  - **Recorder** – captures notes
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~5–7 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

## 🎯 Task Overview

Each group prepares a **short recap** of Week 3, with a different focus:

- **Group 1:** Technology and architecture themes (platform, HANA, cloud, tools)  
- **Group 2:** Design and experience themes (designing solutions, Activate, pitching, Fiori, Coursera links)  

For your focus area:
1. Identify **3–5 key takeaways** from the week
2. Link at least **one takeaway** to what you did in **Coursera** in the afternoon
3. Prepare a short **recap mini‑presentation**

Use:
- Day 1–4 files and topics
- Coursera modules you worked on this week
