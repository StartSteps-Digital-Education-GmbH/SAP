# Day 5: Group Exercise – Fiori App Scenarios
## Two Groups, Two Fiori UX Scenarios

Work in **2 groups**. Each group works on **user experience scenarios** for Fiori apps.

---

## 👥 Group Setup

- Form **2 groups** (each group can have 2–3 members).
- In each group, assign roles:
  - **Facilitator** – guides the discussion and keeps time
  - **Recorder** – captures notes
  - **Presenter** – presents back to the class (~5 minutes)

Write your details:

- **Group number:** _____  
- **Your role:** ___________________________________________

---

## 🎯 Task Overview

Each group designs **Fiori app ideas** for different scenarios:

- **Group 1:** Approval and self‑service scenarios  
- **Group 2:** Reporting / analytics and fact sheet scenarios  

For your scenarios:
1. Decide which **Fiori app type(s)** fit (transactional, analytical, fact sheet)
2. Identify the main **Fiori principles** you would apply
3. Prepare a short **UX description** to share with the class

Use ideas from:
- `01_learning_fiori_simplification_list.md`
- `05_learning_fiori_in_practice.md` (once created)

## 📢 Presenting to the Class

Each **Presenter** should share in about **5 minutes**:
1. The **scenarios** your group worked on
2. The **Fiori app types** you chose and why
3. A brief **UX description** (what users would see and do)

Focus on keeping the story simple and tied to **user roles and tasks**.
